How it works

From platform connection to actionable insight in minutes

01

Connect your platforms

Authenticate with TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube via OAuth. Syntrax requests only the permissions it needs — read access for analytics, write access for publishing.

02

Ingest content signals

Our pipeline pulls performance data at configurable intervals. Per-video metrics are normalized across platforms for direct comparison.

03

Analyze and act

Surface high-performing patterns, schedule follow-up content, and push new videos to all platforms from one interface or via API.

04

Close the loop

Feed performance data back into your content pipeline. Syntrax integrates with your existing tools via webhook or direct API.
