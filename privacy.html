Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Syntrax ("Syntrax," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our platform, website, and services (collectively, the "Services"). By using Syntrax, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register for or use Syntrax, we may collect information you directly provide to us, including:

  • Name and email address when you create an account or request access
  • Payment information processed through secure third-party payment processors
  • Communications you send us, including support requests and feedback
  • Any content or data you upload or submit through the Services

1.2 Information from Third-Party Platforms

When you connect a third-party social media account (such as TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube) to Syntrax, we collect information from those platforms necessary to provide the Services, including:

  • Your platform user ID and basic profile information (display name, profile picture)
  • Access tokens required to authenticate API requests on your behalf
  • Content performance metrics including views, likes, comments, shares, reach, and watch time
  • Audience demographic data made available through platform APIs
  • Publishing status and scheduling information for content you manage through Syntrax

We only request the minimum permissions required to operate the Services. We do not access private messages, contacts, or any data beyond what is necessary for the features you use.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Syntrax, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Pages and features accessed, time spent on pages, and navigation paths
  • Referring URLs and search terms used to find our Services
  • Error logs and diagnostic data to help us identify and fix issues

1.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Syntrax uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our Services. We use session cookies to maintain your authenticated session, preference cookies to remember your settings, and analytics cookies to understand how users interact with the platform. You may control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Syntrax uses the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services, including processing API requests to connected platforms on your behalf
  • Authenticate your identity and authorize access to connected accounts
  • Display analytics dashboards and performance metrics for your content
  • Schedule and publish content to connected platforms as instructed by you
  • Send transactional communications including account notifications, security alerts, and service updates
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve the Services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your data to train machine learning models or sell insights derived from your account data to third parties.

3. How We Share Your Information

Syntrax does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Services, including cloud infrastructure providers, analytics platforms, and payment processors. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

3.2 Third-Party Platforms

When you instruct Syntrax to publish content or retrieve data on your behalf, we transmit the necessary information to the relevant platform APIs (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). This transmission is governed by those platforms' respective privacy policies and terms of service.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Syntrax, our users, or the public.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on the Services before your information is subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Specifically:

  • Account information is retained for the duration of your account and for a reasonable period thereafter to fulfill legal obligations
  • Access tokens for connected platforms are retained only while your account is active and are deleted upon disconnection or account termination
  • Analytics and performance data is retained for up to 24 months to enable historical comparisons
  • Log and diagnostic data is retained for up to 90 days

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

5. Data Security

Syntrax implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit using TLS, secure storage of access tokens, regular security assessments, and access controls limiting who within our organization can access user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

To disconnect a third-party platform account, you may do so through your Syntrax account settings or directly through the relevant platform's privacy settings. To exercise any other rights, contact us at hello@syntrax.io.
